I have worked on an FMCG retail analytics project focused on business performance tracking. I have analysed a dataset of around 47,000 records and integrated category and margin data using Excel lookups. I have performed data preparation by deriving profit, time-based fields like year, month, and quarter, and then built key business metrics such as outlet-wise, category-wise, and region-wise sales and profit, along with monthly trends and top-performing products. I have created interactive pivot dashboards with slicers to enable quick decision-making. The analysis revealed that personal care contributed over 50% of total revenue, Store C underperformed, and perfume was the top-selling product, providing clear, actionable insights for business stakeholders.
